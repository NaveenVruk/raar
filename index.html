The Five Eras

The History of the Known World

What follows is the accepted historical record — from the first days of humanity's divine creation to the present age.

Era One
The First Breath
"In the beginning there was only the Queen, and the Queen breathed, and the world was."
Before the world existed, there was the Queen. The oldest texts do not speak of where she came from — only that she was, and that her existence was the first fact of creation. She was alone for longer than time can measure. From that solitude she made life — creatures shaped in the image of something she carried inside her. These were the first beings on what would become New Meridian. Then she made humanity. Her greatest creation. Her children.
Era Two
The Age of Grace
"The world was generous then. The land gave freely. The air was clean. We did not yet know what waited in the dark."
Humanity flourished on the sacred planet. The Queen walked among her people — not as a ruler but as a presence, felt rather than seen, known rather than proven. The other creatures of the planet — those the Queen had made before humanity — lived alongside them. The people called them the Raars. It was during this age that the great science of AUM was discovered — the substance inside all things, the language of Will, the gift the Queen had woven into the fabric of her world.
Era Three
The Raar Uprising
"They were beautiful. We did not know the beauty was a mask."
The age of peace ended when the true nature of the Raars revealed itself. Beneath their silence was not peace but patience. What followed was a century of war unlike anything humanity had faced. The Raars wielded power over the very fabric of the world — sound, air, earth, time itself. The Queen was their greatest weapon — and their greatest vulnerability. She died, and the world changed forever. Her disciples — the ones who would become the Creators — avenged her. They mastered Will as no human had before.
Era Four
The Ascension
"Eight days. That is all it took. Eight days for love to become a world."
The Creators looked at what remained of humanity and made a decision that no record can fully capture — not because the facts are lost, but because some acts of love are simply beyond the capacity of language. They would give themselves completely. Not to fight. Not to rule. But to become — to transform their Will and their lives into something permanent enough to carry everyone else. In eight days, the moons were built. One after another, the Creators descended into the cores of their creations and became the light that has guided every generation since.
Era Five — Present Day
The Age of the Blessed
"We live in the sky now. As she always intended."
Five thousand years have passed since the Eight Days. The moons have grown into worlds — each one a civilization, each one a culture, each one carrying the Creators' legacy. But something is changing. In the AUM, in the land below, in the dreams of those sensitive enough to feel it. In Year 5000 A.A., a royal makes a move. The sky catches fire. The Age of the Blessed may be ending.

The Language of Will

AUM

The Avogadro-Uhlenbeck-Meitner Particle. The substance inside all things.

"The tissue samples from Subject R-7 continue to defy classification. Every cellular structure we understand is present. Every chemical process we recognize is occurring. And yet the energy readings at the subatomic level do not correspond to any model we brought with us from home. It is as if each atom in this creature contains something extra. Something we have no instrument sensitive enough to measure. Something that, I suspect, is not passive."

— Dr. Asha Meitner-Vael, Personal Research Log, PMY 3
AUM is the subquantum substrate found in all matter on New Meridian — living and inert, native and non-native. It constitutes a universal information layer operating beneath the threshold of observable quantum mechanics. Volitional Force — commonly called Will — is not a phenomenon independent of AUM. It is the primary mechanism by which conscious entities interact with, read, and overwrite AUM-encoded reality.

The implications extend across physics, biology, neuroscience, and the nature of creation itself. The Creators did not merely build the moons. They became them — their Will, permanently encoded into the AUM of each moon's core, sustaining every life lived beneath their artificial skies.
